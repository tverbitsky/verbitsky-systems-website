Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deployment take? ▼
Typical deployment takes 3-5 days for standard installations. This includes server setup, AI model deployment, and initial configuration. Complex integrations may require additional time.
What equipment brands are supported? ▼
We support all major PLC manufacturers including Allen-Bradley, Siemens, Schneider Electric, Mitsubishi, and more. AI can work with any equipment that has documentation available.
Is the system truly air-gapped? ▼
Yes. The entire system runs on your servers with no external connections required. Your data never leaves your facility, ensuring complete privacy and security compliance.
What support is included? ▼
All plans include 24/7 technical support, quarterly system health checks, and unlimited email support. Enterprise plans also include dedicated account management and priority response.
